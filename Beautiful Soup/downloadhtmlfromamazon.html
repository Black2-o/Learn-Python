What's the difference between an iPhone 11 and an iPhone 12?
The iPhone 11 offers the benefits of having the same zoom range, water-resistance, and dual 12MP cameras as the iPhone 12, with the added benefit of coming at a lower cost. Alternatively, the iPhone 12 offers the benefits of 5G cellular connectivity, MagSafe charging compatibility, and a higher screen resolution.

Are all iPhones waterproof?
iPhone models after the iPhone 7 are water resistant but not fully waterproof. The iPhone 11, 12, and 13 are water resistant for up to 30 minutes in 6 meters of water.
see more
What are the benefits of the iPhone 14?
The iPhone 14 features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, an advanced dual-camera system with a 12MP main camera and 12MP ultra-wide camera, 2x optical zoom range, TrueDepth front camera with autofocus, safety features, and a battery life of up to 20 hours. The iPhone 14's long lasting battery makes it the ideal model for daily tasks, capturing photos, and streaming.

What to look for when buying an iPhone?
When purchasing an iPhone, consider the different types of iPhones available, screen size, camera quality, its battery life, and any additional features. The iPhone 14 Pro Max includes a Pro camera system with a 6x optical zoom range, making it ideal for photographers, while the iPhone 14 has a 2x optical zoom range, making it harder to capture long-distance photos.

What is the typical battery life of an iPhone?
The battery life of iPhones varies depending on the model, age of the phone, and daily use; however, most iPhones typically last between 11 and 17 hours. Additionally, iPhones from the iPhone 8 and on support fast charging, meaning that they can be recharged up to 50% in 30 minutes.
